Data Deletion Instructions

If you want your data deleted from AI Growth Platform, follow one of these options:

Option 1: Email Request

  1. Send an email to ccianurag@gmail.com
  2. Subject: Data Deletion Request
  3. Include your account email and (optional) your tenant/company name.

Option 2: In-app (if available)

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.

What happens next

We will verify ownership and delete your account data and connected tokens within a reasonable time.
